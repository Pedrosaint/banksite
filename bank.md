# 🏦 Single-Page Credit Union Website (Inspired Design) — Prompt

## 🎯 Goal

Build a modern, responsive **single-page financial website** inspired by the structure and feel of [Ohio Valley Community Federal Credit Union](https://www.ohiovalleycu.org/?utm_source=chatgpt.com), but with a **unique design, branding, and layout**.

---

## 🧱 Core Requirements

### 1. Single Page Application (SPA)

* The entire website must exist on **one page**
* No route/page navigation (no `/about`, `/loans`, etc.)
* Navigation should use **smooth scrolling** to sections

---

### 2. Navigation Behavior

* Sticky top navigation bar
* Each nav item scrolls to a section:

  * Home
  * About
  * Accounts
  * Loans & Rates
  * Services
  * Contact

👉 Example behavior:

* Clicking **"Loans & Rates"** smoothly scrolls to that section
* Use anchor links or scroll libraries (e.g., `scrollIntoView`, `react-scroll`)

---

### 3. Branding

* DO NOT use the original logo or brand assets
* Create a **new fictional credit union brand**, e.g.:

  * Name: *NovaTrust Credit Union* (or similar)
* Use:

  * Clean, trustworthy color palette (blue, white, green tones)
  * Professional typography

---

## 🎨 UI Sections (All in One Page)

### 🏠 Hero Section

* Headline: Trust-focused financial message
* Subtext explaining services
* CTA buttons:

  * "Open an Account"
  * "Apply for a Loan"
* Background: abstract finance-themed or modern gradient

---

### 📞 Contact Section

### 🔐 Login Panel (UI Only)
